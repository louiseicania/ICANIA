How Vinted reselling works.

Icania is a South West London Vinted reselling service. We list and sell your clothes on Vinted for you, from the first message to the final payment. No upfront cost, no contracts to sign, and no dealing with buyers.

01.

You get in touch.

Submit the enquiry form on the Sell page and we’ll call you within 24 hours. The call is short. We just want to confirm the brands you have, the rough volume, and whether collection or drop off works better for you.

Minimum is ten pieces. We take any brand except Shein, Primark, H&M, Boohoo, and Missguided. Unbranded vintage is welcome.

02.

Clothes are collected.

You can drop your pieces off at our South West London base for free, or we can collect from your home for a small fee that comes out of your final payment.

Every seller’s items go straight into clear, labelled bags on arrival and stay that way until they’re listed. Nothing gets mixed up between batches.

We currently only operate within South West London. If you’re further out and have a sizeable batch, drop us a line. We’ll see what’s possible.

03.

Pieces are shot, listed, sold.

Every item is steamed, photographed against a clean backdrop, measured, and listed on the Icania Vinted shop with a written description. Pricing is set by us based on what comparable items are selling for.

From there, we handle everything: replying to buyer messages, packing, posting, and resolving any issues. You don’t need to be involved at any point.

Most pieces list within a fortnight of collection. We’ll send you a link to the shop so you can see what’s gone live.

04.

Money is split.

Whatever sells, we split fifty fifty after Vinted’s buyer protection fee. We pay out monthly by bank transfer once an item has cleared the buyer’s return window.

If you opted for collection, the agreed fee comes out of your share at the first payout.

If it doesn’t sell.

Items stay listed on the rail for three months. After that, they go to one of two places, and you decide which:

Returned to you.  Pieces come back, washed and folded. You arrange collection or pick them up.

Donated.  Anything you don’t want back is donated to FARA or Oxfam in person. We’ll send you the receipt.

The rule keeps the rail fresh and prevents stock from going stale. It also means sellers aren’t left waiting indefinitely on pieces that aren’t going to move.

Icania was started by Louise, a one woman South West London consignment service for women who have wardrobes full of clothes they no longer wear and no time to sell them.

The business runs on a simple principle: most people who could be making money from their old clothes never will, because the work involved is genuinely tedious. Photographing each piece, writing a description, dealing with messages, packing, posting, handling returns. After two or three items, most people give up.

Icania does the work. You drop your pieces off, or have them collected, and they reappear on the rail, photographed properly, written up properly, and sold to buyers who already trust the shop. Whatever sells, the proceeds are split fifty fifty.

The shop has built up over six hundred and fifty five star reviews on Vinted and a steady base of repeat sellers across South West London. We’re selective about what we accept, the curation is the whole point, but we’re happy to take a look at almost anything.

Privacy policy.

This policy explains what personal information Icania collects, why, how we look after it, and your rights.

Who we are

Icania is a sole trader consignment service based in South West London. Contact: louise@icania.co.uk.

What we collect

When you submit the enquiry form on our Sell page, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your postcode (and full address only after the initial call, if you proceed)
  • Information you give us about the items you’d like to consign, including any photos you upload
  • Email address if you contact us by email

If you proceed with consignment, we additionally collect bank details for payment, kept on file only as long as the consignment relationship is active.

Why we collect it

To respond to your enquiry, manage your consignment, and pay you for items that sell. That’s the only purpose.

How long we keep it

If you submit an enquiry but don’t proceed, we delete your details within 90 days. If you become a seller, we keep your details for as long as the relationship is active and for six years after, in line with HMRC record keeping requirements.

Who we share it with

Nobody. Your details are not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for any reason. The only exception is where we are legally required to disclose information by a court or regulator.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, to restrict or object to its processing, and to data portability. Email louise@icania.co.uk to exercise any of these and we’ll respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office at ico.org.uk.

Cookies

This website uses no analytics cookies, no advertising cookies, and no third party trackers. We don’t need them.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy materially, we’ll update the date at the top. The current version always governs.

Terms.

By using Icania’s consignment service, you agree to the following. We’ll always confirm the specifics on the call before any items change hands.

The agreement

You consign womenswear pieces to Icania for resale on Vinted under the Icania shop account. Icania does not buy your items outright; ownership remains yours until a buyer purchases the piece, at which point ownership transfers to the buyer.

What we accept

Womenswear in clean, sellable condition. Minimum batch of ten pieces. We don’t accept Shein, Primark, H&M, Boohoo, or Missguided. We reserve the right to decline any item we don’t think will sell.

Pricing

Prices are set by Icania based on comparable sold listings and the condition of the item. We may adjust prices during the consignment period to keep pieces moving.

Splits and payment

Whatever an item sells for, after Vinted’s buyer protection fee, is split fifty fifty between you and Icania. Payouts are made by bank transfer monthly, for items that have cleared Vinted’s buyer return window in the previous month.

Collection fees

If you opt for home collection rather than drop off, the agreed fee is deducted from your share at the first payout.

Three month listing period

Items remain listed for three months from their go live date. After that, unsold items are either returned to you or donated to FARA or Oxfam, based on what you tell us at the start of the consignment.

Returns from buyers

If a buyer returns an item, Icania handles the return. The item, if still sellable, will be relisted; you continue to be entitled to your share if it sells subsequently. If a returned item is damaged in transit and Icania has to refund the buyer without recovering the value, the loss is borne by Icania, not by you.

Liability for items in our care

Icania takes reasonable care of your items but is not insured against loss, theft, or damage while pieces are in our possession. By consigning, you acknowledge that the items remain at your risk. We strongly recommend not consigning anything of significant sentimental value.

Authenticity

By consigning a piece, you warrant that it is authentic and that you have the right to sell it. If a piece is later discovered to be counterfeit, you are responsible for any costs Icania incurs as a result, including buyer refunds.

Withdrawing pieces

You can ask to withdraw any unsold piece at any time during the three month period. We’ll remove the listing and arrange for the item to be returned or collected.

Ending the agreement

Either party can end the consignment relationship at any time for any reason. Pieces in our possession will be returned or, if you prefer, donated.

Governing law

These terms are governed by the laws of England and Wales. Any dispute will be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the English courts.

Updates

We may update these terms. The version on this page at the time you submit your enquiry is the version that applies to your consignment.
